# Brand Guidelines - Orango (开个橘子)

## Brand Identity

### Brand Name
- **中文名称**: 开个橘子
- **英文名称**: ORANGO

### Brand Personality
- Friendly and approachable
- Vibrant and energetic
- Playful yet professional

---

## Logo

### Primary Logo
The primary logo consists of:
- The text "开个橘子" or "ORANGO"
- An orange graphic element
- Used as the main brand identifier

### Logo Variations
- **Light Background Version**: Logo on white/light background with orange text
- **Dark Background Version**: Logo on orange/dark background with white/light text
- **Color Variations**: Multiple color combinations available

### Logo Usage Guidelines
- Maintain minimum clear space around logo
- Do not distort or stretch the logo
- Use provided color variations only
- Ensure logo is always legible

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Brand Orange**: Vibrant orange - primary brand color
- **Dark Orange**: Deeper orange tone
- **White**: Clean, neutral base color
- **Light Cream**: Soft, warm background color

### Secondary Colors
- Additional accent colors as shown in color variation versions

### Color Usage
- Use Brand Orange as the primary accent color
- White for backgrounds and text on dark backgrounds
- Use color combinations consistently across all materials

---

## Typography

### Primary Typeface
- Clean, modern sans-serif fonts
- Used for all brand communications

### Typography Usage
- **Headers**: Bold, larger sizes for maximum impact
- **Body Text**: Readable, comfortable sizes
- **Branding Lines**: Playful, expressive typography for taglines
- **Logo Text**: Customized typography for logo

### Typography Hierarchy
- Large display text for main branding
- Medium sizes for section headers
- Smaller sizes for body copy and supporting text

---

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Brand Voice
- Friendly and conversational
- Energetic and enthusiastic
- Approachable and relatable

### Tone Guidelines
- Keep messaging light and positive
- Use playful language when appropriate
- Maintain clarity and professionalism
- Be authentic and genuine

### Brand Messaging Examples
- "This is a branding line saying something hihi hi hi hihihii"
- Use repeating patterns for emphasis and memorability
- Keep taglines concise and memorable

---

## Visual Elements

### Graphic Style
- Clean, modern design aesthetic
- Bold, rounded corners (200px for some elements)
- Consistent spacing and layout

### Image Style
- Use SVG graphics for scalability
- Maintain consistent visual language
- Coordinate with brand color palette

### Layout Principles
- Balanced compositions
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Consistent spacing and alignment

---

## Do's and Don'ts

### Do's
- Use approved color combinations
- Maintain logo proportions
- Keep typography consistent
- Use brand voice in all communications

### Don'ts
- Don't alter logo colors outside approved variations
- Don't stretch or distort logo
- Don't use unapproved fonts
- Don't mix color palettes inconsistently
